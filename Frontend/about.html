🍽️ Welcome to Slurpie

Turning surplus food into smiles, one meal at a time.

🌍 What is Slurpie?

Slurpie is a hyper-local food sharing platform that connects people who have surplus food with nearby individuals who need it. Every day, tons of edible food are wasted while many people struggle to find affordable meals. Slurpie solves this problem by creating a community-driven ecosystem where extra food can be shared instead of thrown away.

Through Slurpie, users can easily list surplus food, discover meals available nearby, and claim them before they expire. By focusing on local connections, Slurpie ensures fast redistribution while strengthening communities.

👨‍💻 Meet Team Slurpie

Slurpie was created by a passionate group of IT students who believe technology can solve real-world problems. Our goal is to reduce food waste and build a community-driven ecosystem where surplus food reaches people who need it most.

🚀 Our Mission

Our mission is to build a sustainable food ecosystem where surplus food reaches the people who need it the most. Slurpie empowers communities to reduce food waste, support local vendors, and encourage responsible consumption.

👥 Who We Help

  • Students and hostel residents
  • Daily wage workers
  • Low income families
  • Local food vendors and cafeterias
  • NGOs and community organisations

✨ Why Slurpie is Different

Unlike traditional food delivery apps, Slurpie focuses on local food sharing. Meals come from nearby people, restaurants, or vendors, making delivery faster and reducing waste. Every order prevents edible food from being discarded while helping someone access affordable meals.
